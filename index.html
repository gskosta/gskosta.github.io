About Me

I am Gauri Shankar. Currently, Junior Researcher, Software Engineering Department, LUT School of Engineering Sciences Lappeenranta, Finland I was a junior research fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, NIT Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India. completed my Master's Degree in Computer Science and Technology (Cyber Security) from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at the Central University of Punjab, under the guidance of Dr. Parvinder Singh, Assistant Professor. I had done my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering from the Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University, Bhilai.  

My current research is focused on blockchain technology and Artificial Intelligence in security and privacy enhancement for decentralized and distributed systems which also include the real time implementation usnig IoT. I am working on blockchain platforms to develop a secure and privacy-oriented decentralised and distributed system. My experience with Blockchain technology dates back to my master's degree, where I developed a privacy-preserving, secure blockchain network for health care, in which access to data is authorized by the patients.
